Following the success of Crisis Wolverine: Insurrection Green, Crisis Wolverine 2: Shadows of the Past is a top-down Role Playing Game for Windows PC. CW2 follows the story of Alex as he discovers the real truth behind Johnny Foreshadow, U of M and those pesky Spartans! CW2 features a bigger cast of characters, memorable villains, familiar (and some unfamiliar) places, a new original soundtrack and art in an interactive RPG World!
